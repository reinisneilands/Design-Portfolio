/About me

I make things simple and beautiful

I'm Reinis, a Senior Designer from Latvia. I believe that powerful digital tools should look stunning and feel intuitive. Drawing from an art school foundation and over five years of scaling products, I specialize in high-fidelity UI, mobile & web experiences, and cohesive design systems. My work is guided by minimalist principles: stripping away the noise to let premium execution and functional elegance take center stage.
